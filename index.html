Howzzat!

How To Play
HOWZATT is a Test against time of your knowledge on the 50 over Format of the Cricket World Cup. The objective is to identify the mystery player, in the shortest possible time.

You need to identify a player who has either been a winning captain, has scored the most runs, taken the most wickets, been the man of the match in the finals or been the man of the series in a particular edition.

You get a total of 40 seconds to identify the player. After every 10 seconds, you get a clue about the mystery player. You lose if the clock winds down!

Use the on-screen keyboard to key in your answer. Hit enter to submit. You can try multiple times. The clock is stopped when you identify the player successfully.

The hints are revealed in the following sequence :-

  1. At the end of 10 seconds, the edition of the World Cup in which the player won the accoldate is revealed.
  2. At the end of 20 seconds, the nationality of the player is revealed.
  3. At the end of 30 seconds, the actual accoldate that the player achievied in that particular edition is revealed.
Legend : WC - Winning Captiain | MR - Most Runs | MW - Most Wickets | MF - MOM in the Finals | MS - Man of the Series
