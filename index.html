Howzzat!

How To Play
HOWZATT is a Test against time of your knowledge on the 50 over Format of the Cricket World Cup. The objective is to identify the mystery player, before the clock runs out.

You need to identify a player who has either been a Winning Captain, has scored the Most Runs, taken the Most Wickets, been the Man of the Match in the Finals or been the Player of the Series in a particular edition.

You get a total of 60 seconds to identify the player. At set intervals, you get a clue about the mystery player. You lose if the clock winds down!

Use the on-screen keyboard to key in your answer. You can have multiple goes and Hit Enter to submit. The clock is stopped when you identify the player successfully.

Pay attention to the number of characters in the players first and last names. This is another clue, that could be of help.

The hints are revealed in the following sequence :-

  1. After 5 seconds, the edition of the World Cup in which the player won the accolade is revealed.
  2. At the end of 15 seconds, the nationality of the player is revealed.
  3. At the end of 25 seconds, the actual accolade that the player achieved in that particular edition is revealed.
  4. At the end of 35 seconds, the first letter of the players first name is revealed.
  5. At the end of 45 seconds, the first letter of the players last name (middle name if applicable) is revealed.
Legend : WC - Winning Captain | MR - Most Runs | MW - Most Wickets | MF - MOM in the Finals | PS - Player of the Series | FN FL - First Name First Letter | LN FL - Last Name First Letter
