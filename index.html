How To Play
GEORDLE is a spin-off on the popular WORDLE game for Geography Lovers. The rules remain mostly the same.

Try names of COUNTRIES or CAPITALS depending on the game type. This is a random selection as it is important to know CAPITALS too :).

Guess the COUNTRY/CAPITAL within the allowed number of tries. Hit the enter button to submit.

Use the Hint button, to reveal the first letter. This can be used only once per game.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Play multiple times a day!
